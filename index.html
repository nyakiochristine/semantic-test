Tafaria Castle Staycation Experience Experience

If you are looking for a get away staycation away from these streets you should definitely consider this breathtaking castle located in the heart of Nyandarua. Something to consider when planning for the trip would be heavy clothes due to the chilly weather that is prevalent almost throughout the year. Also do not leave your cowboy hats, boots and cargo pants since at the castle you will have the chance to ride horses. Another amazing is the fact that there is a museum. I really loved some of the aesthetics of the museum. But how can I forget the amazing scenary views such as the icy Aberdare Ranges from the comfort of your staycation room. Entrance fees are quite affordable. I am assuring you that the place will be worth the stay.

Here are some other cool places to visit in Nyahururu

  • Thompson Falls
  • Aberdare Ranges
  • Lake Olbollosat
  • Subukia Shrine
